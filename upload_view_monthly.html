Upload Monthly PAYE Returns

Follow step-by-step instructions to upload and view

UPLOAD

To Upload monthly PAYE remittance schedule, click on the FILE RETURNS button provided on the dash board.

It leads you to a page where six buttons are provided. Upload monthly PAYE remittance schedule (on the left),View monthly PAYE remittance schedule (on the right). Click on Monthly PAYE remittance schedule

On the upload monthly PAYE remittance schedule page, there are buttons to:

  • Select month – this provides you with a dropdown which you select the month,year you intend to upload for, select station, select payment method.
  • Choose csv file – Click on CHOOSE FILE to upload the file already entered correctly by the taxpayer. Please note that a sample is shown on the page. Download csv file button is provided below the page. This is to ensure taxpayers gets the correct format to file in the monthly PAYE remittance schedule.
  • After choosing the correct file, click on UPLOAD. The app will then show you the status of your filing by displaying successful or indicating an error message(s) if there is/are any. If upload is successful, the user will be redirected to the payment page.

VIEW

To view monthly PAYE remittance schedule uploaded, simply click on VIEW monthly PAYE remittance schedule (by the right) on the upload/view page.

It leads you to monthly PAYE remittance schedule page; you get the button to select the year and month you want to view or previously uploaded. Select the year and month and click SEARCH.
