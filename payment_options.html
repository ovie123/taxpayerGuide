MAKING PAYMENT

Payments can be made online or through the bank. Please note that all payments must be made through Remita or eTransact.

Online Payment

If uploads are successful, you will be redirected to the payment page. Click on payment period.This leads you to a page where you will see details of the payment period selected.

  • Pay – This button is to continue payment online.
  • Update Payment Ref – This is to update ref number for payments made through the bank.
  • Delete - To delete records.

To continue with online payment, click on PAY.

You will be provided with dropdowns where you can select payment method, tax station. After selections have been made, click on PROCEED.

The previous action will lead you to the payment service provider(Remita/eTransact) online payment confirmation page. Complete the form or edit form data and click on make payment

On the payment service provider page, click on SUBMIT.

This action leads you to the page where you can enter your card details and click on PAY.

This action leads you to the page where you can enter your one time password and click on PAY.

This page shows the status of your payment by the payment service(Remita/eTransact).

Bank Payment

Payments through bank must be done through Remita or eTransact with your KGTIN. This payment can be made before or after uploading schedules. After payment have been made, Update the payment Ref number. To update payment Ref, click on PAYMENT button on the side menu. Two dropdowns are provided.

  • Pending Invoices –This option is to make payment, edit ref number or delete a record.
  • Paid Invoices – This option is to view paid invoices history and print receipt.

Click on PENDING INVOICES to navigate to the payment page. Select payment period that payment have been made for. Buttons are provided on the payment history page to PAY, UPDATE and DELETE records.

Buttons are provided on the payment history page to PAY, UPDATE and DELETE records. Click on UPDATE PAYMENT REF.

It provides you with dropdowns where you can enter the ref number and click on GO.

If successful, you will be prompted else an error message and instructions on how to resolve it will be displayed.
