MAKING PAYMENT

Payments can be made online or through the bank.

Online Payment

If uploads are successful,you will be redirected to this page.Click on make payment on the paystack confirmation page to proceed or Click on edit payment data to edit payment information.

You will be redirected to a page where you can enter your credit card details.

Enter your card details and wait for payment authentication. If successful, you wll be redirected to a page where you can print your receipt.Click on print receipt to print your receipt.If not successful, you will be notified of the error message.

Bank Payment

To pay at the bank, print your Invoice and proceed to any designated bank branch to pay.

It leads you to monthly PAYE remittance schedule page; you get the option to select the month you want to view or uploaded. Select the year and click SEARCH.
