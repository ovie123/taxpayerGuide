Introduction: Easy Management of PAYE and Withholding Tax

A step-by-step guide to filing returns / schedules.

OVERVIEW

The PIT (Personal Income Tax) portal is designed to automate payment, filing of PAYE and Withholding taxes. Taxpayers get to see their tax history and can print receipt from this site.

This guide will walk you through the steps to use the PIT portal. A document template for download is provided; taxpayers can choose convenient payment option of their choice.
